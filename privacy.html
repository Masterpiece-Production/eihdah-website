This Policy applies to eihdah.com (the "Site") and any related services that link to it. By accessing or using the Site you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy.

6. Data Security & Retention

We maintain industry‑standard safeguards (encryption in transit, least‑privilege access, regular audits) to protect your information. We retain Personal Information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy or as required by law.
